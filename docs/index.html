Who are we?

AgriFoodure is an online intuitive ecommerce platform developed adopting a circular economy model where producers will have the opportunity to allocate their overproduction therefore significantly contributing to reduce the organic waste. Producers and consumers will sell and buy products through AgriFoodure, facilitating cooperation and increasing their revenue.

We strive to minimize food waste from its origin. Agrifoodure believes in the reuse of energy, so even the organic food waste at the end of the food supply chain [households, restaurants...] will be collected, turned into compost and sent back to the farms to be used as fertilizers, creating a circular motion, reducing food waste at the beginning and the end of the supply chain.
